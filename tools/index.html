BoB - Batch Operating Bot

BoB or Batch Operating Bot is a small .exe file, written in batch. Its objective is to help you getting hands on your Windows and to take care of it in all security. BoB can do many things: detect bugs your disk, delete files, view internet connections and show very important info on your computer! Click to download now!


Rec_a_MT : Minetest recorder

Rec_a_MT is my first Client Side Mod for Minetest! It allows you to record your minetest window by taking screenshots at regular intervals. It also possess a small GUI with many parameters such as FPS or record time. It can be used to do gifs or videos.
